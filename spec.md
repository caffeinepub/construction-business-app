# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build an initial construction business management app (BuildPro Manager) with a dashboard, project management, task management, and client directory, styled with a bold industrial theme.

**Planned changes:**
- Dashboard home screen showing counts of active/completed/total projects, upcoming tasks with due dates, and key business metrics
- Project management (create, view, edit, delete) with fields: name, description, status (Planning / In Progress / Completed), start date, end date, and budget
- Task management within each project (add, edit, complete, delete) with fields: title, assignee name, due date, and status (To Do / In Progress / Done)
- Client directory (add, view, edit, delete) with fields: name, phone, email, and associated projects
- Persistent left sidebar navigation with icons linking to Dashboard, Projects, Tasks, and Clients
- Dark charcoal and amber/orange industrial visual theme with card-based layouts and bold typography applied consistently across all pages
- Logo displayed in the sidebar/top navigation and hero banner displayed on the dashboard, loaded from static assets

**User-visible outcome:** Users can manage construction projects, tasks, and clients through a polished industrial-themed app with a sidebar navigation and an informative dashboard overview.
